# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We expect everyone who participates in our community to uphold this Code of Conduct.

## Our Values

- **Respectful**: We treat everyone with respect and dignity
- **Inclusive**: We welcome people of all backgrounds and experiences
- **Collaborative**: We value diverse perspectives and ideas
- **Professional**: We maintain professionalism in all interactions

## Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:
- Be respectful and courteous
- Listen actively to others
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards others

## Unacceptable Behavior

We will not tolerate:
- Harassment, discrimination, or bullying
- Offensive comments related to gender, sexual orientation, disability, mental illness, neuro(a)typicality, physical appearance, body size, age, race, or religion
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Violent threats or language
- Intimidation or stalking
- Any other conduct that violates laws or professional standards

## Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience a violation of this Code of Conduct, please report it to the maintainers at conduct@example.com. All reports will be kept confidential.

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:
- Warnings
- Temporary bans from participation
- Permanent removal from the community
